# AcadMix Service Level Agreement (SLA)

## 1. Uptime Commitment
AcadMix provides a commercially reasonable **99.9% uptime commitment** for enterprise deployments on the core academic and administrative APIs. 
This equates to a maximum allowable downtime of ~43.8 minutes per month.

## 2. Maintenance Windows
Planned maintenance will occur during off-peak hours (Sunday 00:00 - 04:00 IST) and will not exceed 2 hours per month. Tenants will be notified 72 hours in advance.

## 3. Support Response Times
For enterprise tenants, incidents are triaged based on severity:
- **P0 (Total Outage):** 1 hour response, 4 hour resolution target.
- **P1 (Core Feature Broken):** 4 hour response, 24 hour resolution target.
- **P2 (Non-critical Error):** 12 hour response, next patch resolution.
- **P3 (Feature Request):** 24 hour response, roadmap evaluation.

## 4. Disaster Recovery
- **RTO (Recovery Time Objective):** 4 hours
- **RPO (Recovery Point Objective):** 15 minutes (via continuous Write-Ahead Log archiving to object storage).

For current system status, visit the status page or contact your dedicated AcadMix Enterprise Success Manager.
